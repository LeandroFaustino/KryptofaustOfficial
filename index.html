My Work

Leandro Faustino, aka Kryptofaust, is a young aspiring musician on his journey towards performative excelence where he values virtuosity, creativity, and different musical contexts exploration.

His thirst for music progress has made him reach for the fountains of Metal, Classical, and Jazz music.
His ambition has brought him to perform with Dallian and record two well regarded albums.
